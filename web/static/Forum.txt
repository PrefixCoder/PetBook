Well done,let's get sterted with forum then.

1) If you are interested in any of topics displayed on your screen, press on its name 
and you will be redirected at the discussion page.

2) If none of them interests you, create your own topic!
To make it done you just need to find a window "Add topic" in the left corner of your page.
Type the title in the needed gap and the description of an issue that bothers you.
Then press "Create".
Congratulations, you have just created your own topic! Good job!

3) Each topic consists of title,description and an information about the author.
To find a topic of the needed user is easy. Just look at the email and nickname below the description.
