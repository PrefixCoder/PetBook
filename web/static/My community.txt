Here you have two directions to choose: followers and following

Following are the people you follow
Followers are the people who follow you

You can text both of them and follow your followers

To get this done, you just need to press the button "Follow"
If you don't want to follow this person anymore, press "Unfollow"

If you want to message a certain person, press the button "Message"
You will be redirected to the chat

To enter your followers/following page,just press on the nickname and here you go.
