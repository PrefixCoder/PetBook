There is a lot of stuff on this page, isn`t it?

Well, this is your main page!
You can change your data, write anything you want in your blog,delete your blogs and browse any other page you want.
You can also do it from any other page

To create a record in your blog you need to
1) Fill a big empty gap below the "Blog"

2) Press "Send" and the record will be created

To delete the needed record just hit the cross in the right top corner and the confirm it.

You can also see your gallery above the Blog

If you want to see the picture, just press on it and the picture will become bigger.

If you want to log out, just hit "Logout" on the navbar. It's simple
